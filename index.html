The event, organized by the university's student council, aimed to celebrate local talent and foster a sense of community among the student body. Behind-the-Scenes Glimpses

On April 17th, GDSC AASTU hosted the AASTU Tech Fest 2024 in collaboration with AASTU, CGI AASTU, and Ahamnes Space Science Club. The event featured a captivating speech by university president Dr. Derege, a student Hackathon showcase, and an engaging panel discussion on tech's impact in Africa with notable speakers like Solomon Kassa and Addis Alemayehou.
