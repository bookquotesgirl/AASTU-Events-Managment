Audience Favorite

On a breezy Friday evening, AASTU University transformed into a vibrant hub
of music and camaraderie as students gathered for the much-anticipated campus
concert. The event, organized by the university’s student council, aimed to
celebrate local talent and foster a sense of community among the student body. What started as a modest gathering quickly turned into an unforgettable experience, making it a favorite among students compared to other events held on campus.

Behind-the-Scenes
Glimpses

On April 17th, GDSC AASTU hosted the AASTU Tech Fest 2024 in collaboration with AASTU, CGI AASTU, and Ahamnes Space Science Club. The event featured a captivating speech by university president Dr. Derege, a student Hackathon showcase, and an engaging panel discussion on tech's impact in Africa with notable speakers like Solomon Kassa and Addis Alemayehou. The Appreciation and Impact Awards honored influential figures, including Dr. Derege and students like Dagmawi Esayas. Networking sessions followed, along with a second panel featuring tech experts. The deputy minister of innovation and technology, Dr. Yeshurun Alemayehu, shared insights on success. The CGI Club showcased impressive 3D art achievements, and Ahamenes Space Science Club presented their projects. The event concluded with Hackathon winners being announced and prizes awarded. Special thanks were extended to sponsors, guests, and attendees for their support in making the event a success.
